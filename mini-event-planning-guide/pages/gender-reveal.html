Gender Reveal Event Planning

A gender reveal is one of the most exciting milestones for expecting parents, filled with anticipation and joy. At Choi & Co. Events, we specialize in creating magical and creative ways to share this special news with your family and friends. Whether you want a grand surprise reveal or a simple, intimate moment, our team ensures every detail is perfectly planned. From fun reveal activities and themed decorations to customized cakes and interactive experiences, we tailor each event to match your vision. Let us help you turn this heartfelt celebration into a day filled with excitement, laughter, and cherished memories through our unique packages.

Balloon & Smoke Reveals

Add a dramatic touch with balloons or colored smoke that fills the air in pink or blue, creating an exciting and picture-perfect moment for everyone to enjoy.

Cake & Dessert Reveals

Sweeten the surprise with a custom cake or dessert table where the first slice or bite reveals the baby’s gender in a fun and delicious way.

Themed Parties

Celebrate with style using creative themes, from rustic chic to whimsical fairytale setups, making your gender reveal as unique as your family’s story.
