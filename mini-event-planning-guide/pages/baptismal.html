Baptismal Planning

Baptism is one of life’s most treasured milestones—a sacred moment of welcoming a child into the Christian faith and into the loving embrace of family and friends. At Choi & Co. Events, we provide complete baptismal planning services, from church coordination to reception styling. Our packages ensure that every detail, big or small, is handled with care and devotion. Explore our baptismal categories below and let us help you celebrate this beautiful occasion with elegance, joy, and heartfelt memories.

Traditional Baptism

We assist in organizing traditional baptismal ceremonies in partnership with your chosen church. From preparation of essentials such as candles, certificates, and attire, to arranging the reception, our team ensures a seamless and meaningful event for the whole family.

Themed Reception

After the sacred rites, the celebration continues with a beautifully styled reception. Choose from a variety of themes—angelic, pastel, floral, or modern minimalist—to match your family’s preferences. Decorations, catering, and entertainment are all tailored to create an unforgettable experience.

Personalized Packages

For families who want something unique, we offer personalized baptismal packages. This includes custom invitations, photo and video coverage, special keepsakes, and thoughtful details that reflect your family’s story and faith journey. Every element is carefully planned to make your celebration truly one of a kind.
