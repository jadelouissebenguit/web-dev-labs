Our Story

Dylan's bakeshop was born from the thought of having a cozy place that is healing. Founded in 2025 by Ms. Jade Benguit, a student who enjoys sweets and bakes as a hobby. With a passion for creating delightful treats, Dylan's Bakeshop aims to provide a warm and inviting atmosphere for everyone to enjoy.
